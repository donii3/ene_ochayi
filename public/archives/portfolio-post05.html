Hopefull Veronica

Location: IDP Camp, Benue State.
Service: PhotoShoot
Date: October '2023

Details

Veronica Unenge is a widow and a mother of 10 children living as a refugee in an internally Displaced Camp in Benue State,Nigeria. she and other widows living in the IDP camp had lost thier husbands to the communal clash which left them to forcefully vacate their homes and everything they had worked for over the years which includes their farms which is their major source of livelihood.

The communial attacks come from herders who go into the farms of people with their cattle leaving the farm in a shadow of itself thereby rubbishing the toil of the farmer. The protest of the community of farmers caused the herders to retaliate with series of attacks killing many.

Read more.

Veronica had since lefft smoking since her teenage age, but she got back to smoking as she can no longer stop because it gives her comfort, life had not been fair to her. Most of the widows have no access to jobs and some of them resort to begging just to feed their children. Veronica says she is not willing to give up on life yet.
